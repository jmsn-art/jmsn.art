Available Licensing

  • Editorial Use – magazines, blogs, news, or non-commercial features.
  • Commercial Use – advertising, merchandise, branding, or promotional campaigns.
  • Media & Design – album covers, book jackets, film/TV, or digital installations.
  • Custom Agreements – tailored licenses for unique projects.

How to Request a License

Please email contact@jmsn.art with details about your project:

  • Which artwork(s) you’d like to use
  • Type of usage (editorial, commercial, etc.)
  • Duration and region of usage
  • Your organization or project info

You will receive a clear agreement outlining rights, duration, fees, and any usage restrictions.
